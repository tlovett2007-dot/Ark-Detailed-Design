Deck Heights

Total internal height: 15.54m less roof zone (3.2m) less ballast zone (2.0m) = 10.34m for three decks. Divided by 3 = ~3.45m clear per deck (before deck structure thickness of ~0.5m each = ~2.95m net clear). Top deck has additional headroom from roof slope geometry.

Deck numbering convention: Maritime bottom-up — Deck 1 (bottom/Lowers), Deck 2 (middle/Twos), Deck 3 (top/Threes). Matches Genesis Hebrew: tachtiyyim (Lowers), sheniyyim (Twos), shelishim (Threes). Note: "Lowers" not "Firsts" — positional not ordinal — suggesting the reference point is the entry door at Deck 2 level. See Biblical Analysis.
