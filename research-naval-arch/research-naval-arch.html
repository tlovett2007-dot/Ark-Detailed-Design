1. Overview

This research project constitutes a rigorous naval architecture feasibility study of Noah's Ark as described in Genesis 6–8. The work applies professional naval architecture methodology — hull form analysis, stability, seakeeping, structural design, systems engineering — to the biblical account, treated as a primary technical specification.

The project has reached a stage where formal academic collaboration would significantly strengthen both the methodology and the publication pathway. The Australian Maritime College (AMC), University of Tasmania, is the natural Australian partner for this work.
